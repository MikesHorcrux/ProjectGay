# VolunQueer Agents

## Overview

VolunQueer connects the right volunteers with LGBTQ+ events, streamlines management, and showcases program impact with customized, end-to-end support.

---

## User Agents

### Event Organizer
- Creates and manages LGBTQ+ events
- Defines volunteer requirements (roles, skills, availability)
- Reviews and approves volunteer applications
- Communicates with volunteers before, during, and after events
- Tracks event metrics and generates impact reports

### Volunteer
- Browses and discovers LGBTQ+ events needing support
- Creates a profile with skills, interests, and availability
- Applies to volunteer at events
- Logs hours and contributions
- Receives recognition and tracks personal impact

### Program Administrator
- Oversees multiple events and organizers
- Manages organization-wide volunteer pool
- Generates aggregate impact reports
- Configures platform settings and permissions
- Ensures compliance and safety standards

---

## AI Agents

### Volunteer Matcher
- Analyzes volunteer profiles and event requirements
- Suggests optimal volunteer-event pairings
- Considers skills, location, availability, and past performance
- Learns from successful matches to improve recommendations

### Schedule Optimizer
- Balances volunteer availability across events
- Prevents over-commitment and burnout
- Fills coverage gaps proactively
- Handles last-minute changes and substitutions

### Impact Reporter
- Aggregates volunteer hours and contributions
- Generates visual impact dashboards
- Creates shareable reports for stakeholders and donors
- Identifies trends and growth opportunities

### Communication Assistant
- Drafts event announcements and reminders
- Personalizes outreach to volunteers
- Handles common questions and FAQs
- Escalates complex inquiries to organizers
